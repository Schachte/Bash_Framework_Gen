Navigating a structured web site:
As an example, suppose that at the top level there are two portfolios

The top or Home level has two portfolios:
        1) Pictures of Daniel Jacob
        2) Pictures of Vacations

In turn, the portfolio of Daniel Jacob pictures has two sub portfolios
        1.1) Pictures of DJ @ 0 months old
        1.2) Pictures of DJ @ 2 months old

And the Vacations portfolio has two sub portfolios:
        2.1) Pictures of your Honeymoon
        2.2) Pictures of your trip to Seattle

So, starting at your top level, say it is called:

        ~/www/port2

There would be the following directory structure:

        ~/www/port2/
                Danel_Jacob
                Vacations

Expanding even further:

        ~/www/port2/
                Daniel_Jacob/
                        DJ_month_00/
                        DJ_month_02/
                Vacations/
                        Honeymoon/
                        Seattle/

Expanding even futher:

        ~/www/port2/
                Daniel_Jacob/
                        DJ_month_00/
                                0000.html
                                000N.html
                                etc/
                                images/
                                m4/
                                index.html
                        DJ_month_02/
                                0000.html
                                000N.html
                                etc/
                                images/
                                m4/
                                index.html
                Vacations/
                        Honeymoon/
                                0000.html
                                000N.html
                                etc/
                                images/
                                m4/
                                index.html
                        Seattle/

                                0000.html
                                000N.html
                                etc/
                                images/
                                m4/
                                index.html
                                
Now, collapsing back one level, you need to have a port.conf
file that is the source of some program that will create
and index.html file that will let you navigate to various levels.
Put a port.conf file at each location specified below:

        ~/www/port2/
                port.conf <--   Home level
                                Has links down to

                                ./Daniel_Jacob/index.html
                                ./Vacations/index.html

                index.html      generated from ./port.conf
                Daniel_Jacob/
                        port.conf <-- 2nd Level
                                        Has links up to Home Level:
                                        ../index.html

                                        and down to:

                                        ./DJ_month_00/index.html
                                        ./DJ_month_02/index.html

                        index.html      generated from ./port.conf
                        DJ_month_00/
                                index.html
                        DJ_month_02/
                                index.html
                Vacations/
                        port.conf <-- 2nd Level
                                        Has links up to Home Level:
                                        ../index.html

                                        and down to:


                                        ./Honeymoon/index.html
                                        ./Seattle/index.html

                        index.html      generated from ./port.conf
                        Honeymoon/
                                index.html
                        Seattle/
                                index.html

So, at the Top or Home Level,

portfolioNavigation:Home

At the second level:

portfolioNavigation:LINK([[Home]], [[../index.html]]) | BASENAME([[$PWD]])
